Scratch for ML

Browser playground за 2D classification, вдъхновен от TensorFlow Playground.

  • Поне 3 live controls: activation, learning rate, hidden layers, noise, features.
  • Трябва да се вижда decision boundary, loss в real-time.
  • Предавате demo, README.md, design-notes.md.
